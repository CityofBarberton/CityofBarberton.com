Barberton Leaf Removal

The leaf removal schedule for this year will be posted to the city's calendar

Please note the dates/schedule for each ward. Keep in mind that as the weather gets worse, and the leaves fall faster, it may take longer to return to your area.

The following will help the City provide you with better service.

  • Rake leaves into a row and placed in the street no more than two (2) feet from the curb/edge of the street without obstructing traffic. Please DO NOT block any drains as this can create flooding.
  • The leaves must be out prior to the day that is scheduled for your ward. Please do not wait until the equipment/crew is in your ward.
  • Do NOT put branches, sticks, dirt, rocks, flowers or shrubbery clippings out. This can cause damage to the equipment.

The sweeper will be pushing the leaves between the hours of 11:00 pm and 7:00 am. Cars parked on the street between these hours will receive tickets according to Ordinance #6-1993.

For those residents who wish to have immediate service, the City is offering the following options:

  • Bagged leaves may be taken to: Mulch Makers of Ohio, located at 3307 Clarks Mill Road. Their hours of operation are Monday - Friday 7:30 am to 5:00 pm. They are requesting that you stop at the main office so that they may direct you to where to put the leaves. You will have to de-bag the leaves yourself, which allows you to re-use your bags.
  • Bagged leaves may also be brought to the following drop off areas: Newton Park, Edgewood Park (27th St NW parking lot) and Tuscora Park. If you wish to re-use your bags, you may dump out your leaves in the "loose pile" or just place the bagged leaves in the appropriate area. Signs will be posted.
