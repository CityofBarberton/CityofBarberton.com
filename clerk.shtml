Barberton Clerk of Courts

Thank you for using our online web resources.

The Barberton Municipal Court provides justice services for the communities of Barberton, Green, New Franklin, Norton, Copley, Coventry and Clinton.

Our Court handles misdemeanor traffic and criminal cases, as well as civil complaints up to $15,000 and small claims complaints up to $3,000. Preliminary hearings for felony cases are also conducted here.

The Barberton Municipal Clerk of Courts office is responsible for maintaining a public record of all court cases in Barberton Municipal Court as well as collecting and distributing fines and fees associated with these cases.

The Clerk of Court’s website (www.cityofbarberton.com/clerkofcourts) will help you find answers to your questions, search court case information, pay fines online, and view the docket of the court’s hearings.

The Clerk of Courts office is committed to providing you with professional, efficient, and friendly service and the continued improvement of those services.
