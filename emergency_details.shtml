2013-07-15 2:30 PM

Red Cross Shelter

The Red Cross is operating a shelter at the YMCA building located at 500 W. Hopocan Avenue.

The following services are available: Warm Shower, Limited Cleaning Kits, Quiet Area, and Food.

2013-07-15 11:00 AM

Donations for Flood Victims

Anyone wishing to make a monetary donation to support victims of the recent flooding in Barberton can do so in person at the Lake Anna YMCA, the Barberton Active adult Center, or via the online donations page.

2013-07-13 1:00 PM

Volunteers

The City has Volunteers handing out flood packets to the rest of South Barberton from VanBuren to Ohio and Snyder to Elson Pointe. Our staff has already been out to some of the obscure flooding locations to distribute information. Volunteers from the Chapel group are stationed at the Slovene Center on 14th and will be there from 9–4 today, 1-5 Sunday, and Monday evening. Over fifty volunteers are serving warm food and helping the neighbors move flood damaged stuff out to curb. Volunteers are Identified by bright orange vests. The Red Cross is moving into the area and coordinating with the Chapel group to drop off water and complement each other’s services.

Safety

Barberton Police are doing extra patrols in the flood areas due to individuals scavenging flood items which have been put out on the curbs.

Trash

The service department is out in the community assisting citizens. Republic Waste will be dropping of at least one dumpster on 14th & Tusc and one dumpster on Stanley and George. Dumpster will be on the roadway and marked with safety cones. Republic is working to have two extra trucks mobilized for curb pick up Monday morning.

Programs

The City has been taking calls and meeting people all morning, and the Mayor has been out in the affected neighborhoods assisting people. The assistance center at the CIty Building closed at 12:30 today and will reopen on Monday at 8AM. Sanitizer will be being dropped of to families who have requested it as well as to the volunteer stations.

If you or someone you know needs assitance please be sure to call (330) 861-7279 so that we can provide prompt assistance.
