Court Forms

These forms are provided for the convenience of residents of the Barberton Municipal Court District.

REMEMBER - legal matters are often complicated and involve issues that are best handled by an attorney. If you need more information contact the Akron Bar Association. The Akron Bar Association's Lawyer Referral Service can provide you with the name of an attorney who will do an initial one-half hour consultation with you for $30.00. The Lawyer Referral Service can be reached by telephone at (330) 253-5038 or by e-mail at lris@akronbar.org.
