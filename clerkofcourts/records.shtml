Public Records

The Clerk of Courts office provides public viewing and/or copies of court records at 576 W Park Avenue, Room 205, Barberton, OH 44203, Monday through Friday between the hours of 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m., with the exception of published holidays. We highly recommend obtaining records from 1996 to present on our website, where you can get immediate access. If you are looking for records prior to 1996, submit a written Public Records Request Form, by e-mail, mail or in-person.

Public Records Copy Fees

Certified Copies $1.00
Computer Docket Printouts (per page) / Copies (per page) $0.10
