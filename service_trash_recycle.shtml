Bulk Collection "Tag" Fees

These charges are only for additional items that are not placed inside the trash cart. Residents that have items that will not fit in the cart should purchase stickers at the various local vendors in Barberton at least 24 hours before their collection day. Stickers should be placed, in a conspicuous place, on the item to be collected. Items placed at curb without stickers will not be collected with regular collection and may be assessed an additional charge if a return trip is required.
