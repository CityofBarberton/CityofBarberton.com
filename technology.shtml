Barberton Information Technology Department

The primary purpose of the City of Barberton's Information Technology department is to provide the City's departments with cohesive and cost effective technologies which will help them increase their operational efficiency. The IT department accomplishes this by blending IT service management best practices, such as those outlined in the Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL), with agile Software Engineering techniques. This blend of methodologies is designed to yield a flexible yet reliable and secure infrastructure which can be maintained with minimal cost.
