Holiday Lights

The City of Barberton hosts a display of multi-colored holiday lights as part of the annual Lake Anna Holiday Light Display at Lake Anna Park in Barberton.

The lights will remain lit through New Years Day. Lights will be turned on at 5:00 p.m. and remain on until 9:00 p.m. Sunday through Thursday evenings. Lights will remain lit until 10:00 p.m. on Fridays, Saturdays and holidays.

Each of the corners of Lake Anna is decorated in a particular theme.

There is no charge to see the brilliant display, which is a cooperative effort between the City of Barberton and the Barberton Community Foundation.

Lake Anna Park is located at 615 W Park Avenue, between West Park Avenue and West Lake Avenue. For more information, contact Barberton Beautification at 330-848-6653.
