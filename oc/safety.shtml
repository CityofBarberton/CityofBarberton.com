Barberton Safety Director

The Safety Director is the head of the Department of Public Safety and is appointed by the Mayor. According to the City Charter, the Safety Director is the supervising authority responsible for the Police and Fire Divisions.


Request for No Parking, STOP and other signage

To make a formal Request for No-Parking, STOP and Other Signage with the City of Barberton, please fill out the attached form and submit to the Safety Director by email at safety@cityofbarberton.com or mail-in hard copy to The City of Barberton, Attn: Safety Director, 576 West Park Avenue, Barberton, OH 44203.
