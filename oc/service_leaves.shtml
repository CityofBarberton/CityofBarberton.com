Barberton Leaf Removal

The City of Barberton will begin picking up loose leaves beginning October 20th, 2014 and will continue for at least 6 weeks depending on Mother Nature’s cooperation. To facilitate pickup, leaves must be raked into a row and placed on the street no more than two (2) feet from the curb or the edge of the street without obstructing traffic. Please note the dates listed below that we are scheduled to be in each Ward and keep in mind that as the weather gets worse and the leaves fall faster it may take longer for us to return to your area. The leaves must be out prior to the day we are scheduled to be in your Ward. Please do not wait until you see the equipment/crews in your Ward.

Please note that the 3rd shift equipment operator will push leaves into piles between 11:30 pm and 7:30 am for crews to pick-up the following day. Cars parked on the streets between these hours on the scheduled day to push leaves are subject to ticketing for violating ordinance #6-1993

For those residents that wish to have immediate service we are offering the following options:

  1. You may take your bagged leaves to Mulch Makers of Ohio located at 3307 Clark Mill Rd. Their hours are Monday – Friday 7:30 am to 5:00 pm. Please stop in the office and they will direct you to the location on their property for you to de-bag the leaves yourself. They must be de-bagged, which allows you to re-use your bags.
  2. Bagged leaves may also be brought to the following drop off locations: Newton Park, Edgewood Park (27th St. NW parking lot) and Tuscora Park. If you wish to re-use your bags you may dump out your leaves in the “loose pile” or just place the bagged leaves in the appropriate marked area. Signs will be posted.
