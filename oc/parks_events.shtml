Annual Day of Play

Saturday, June 6 , 12:00 p.m.—3:00 p.m. Join us for a “A Day of Play” at one of our four parks ~ Tuscora, Avilee Webb, Edgewood, or Lake Anna. The day will include food, games, fun and inflatables. Activities at Lake Anna will include PWC instruction and rides. You will also have the opportunity to learn about many of the great programs offered by Barberton Parks & Recreation.

Larry Bidlingmyer Community Bike Ride

July 11, 2015 Registration from 8:30-9:30 a.m. ; Ride @ 10 a.m. Bring your bike, helmet and your friends to ride from the Snyder Ave. trailhead in Barberton to the Manchester Rd. trailhead in Akron, and back! Snacks and a light lunch are provided following the ride. Register by calling 330.861.7138.
