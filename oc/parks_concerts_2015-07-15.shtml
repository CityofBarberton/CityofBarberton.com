Matt Schafer Polka Band Polka

For Matt Schafer, polka music has, is, and always will be a huge part of his life. He expresses his love for the genre every Saturday as a host on the 88.1 radio station, Saturday Polka. Extending his passion, he puts on a fantastic show of classic polka that the audience is sure to tap their feet along to!
