Barberton Income Tax

The City of Barberton income tax rate is currently 2.25%.

Income taxes for the City of Barberton are processed by CCA (Central Collection Agency) of Cleveland, Ohio. For all tax returns and registration forms, please go to www.ccatax.ci.cleveland.oh.us.

Due to a change in state law, city income tax returns must now be filed by April 15th.
