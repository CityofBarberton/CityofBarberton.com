Lake Anna Park Concert Series

The Barberton Parks Department presents 2014 Summer Concert Series at Lake Anna Park. The Concerts are held rain or shine. In the event of thunder, lightning, or heavy rains, please call the automated line at (330) 848-6784 for up to the date cancellation information.

Chairs are not permitted in the park until 6:00AM the day of the concert.
